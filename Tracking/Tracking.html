Tracking with a Kalman filter

move mouse around in gray area

For the curious:

This Kalman filter uses a GPS-like sensor, which provides x and y positional data from a circular gaussian with a mean at the true mouse position and radial variance of ~50 pixels, updated at 20 Hz. The filter uses that data to estimate position, velocity, and acceleration of the mouse pointer. The filter's control input is ignored; instead, the movement of the mouse is accommodated by callibrating process noise.
